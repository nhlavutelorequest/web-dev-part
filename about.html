Our Journey

Founded in 2015 by a passionate team of IT professionals who recognized the growing need for reliable and accessible technology support in their community, TechFix Solutions began as a modest neighborhood tech support service with a simple mission: to make technology work for everyone.

2015

The Beginning

TechFix Solutions was founded by three computer science graduates with a shared vision of democratizing technology support. Starting from a small office in Johannesburg, we began offering basic computer repair services.

2017

First Major Expansion

We expanded our service portfolio to include network setup and cybersecurity consultations. Our team grew to 10 dedicated professionals, and we opened our second location in Cape Town.

2019

Enterprise Solutions Launch

Launched our enterprise solutions division, securing our first major corporate clients. We introduced 24/7 support services and achieved our first industry certifications.

2021

Digital Transformation

Pivoted to include cloud computing services and remote support solutions during the pandemic. Helped over 200 businesses transition to remote work environments successfully.

2023

AI Integration & Innovation Hub

Established our Innovation Hub and began integrating AI-powered solutions into our service offerings. Launched our mobile app for real-time support and monitoring.

2025

Celebrating a Decade of Excellence

Today, we serve over 2500 clients across multiple provinces, maintain a 99.8% uptime guarantee, and continue to innovate in emerging technologies like IoT and edge computing.
